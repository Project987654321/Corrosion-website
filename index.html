Q1. Identify the corrosion type

Iron rusts uniformly when exposed to moist air.

Uniform corrosion occurs evenly over the entire surface.

Q2. Is this wet or dry corrosion?

Rusting of iron in the presence of water and oxygen.

Wet corrosion involves an electrolyte such as water.

Q3. Identify the corrosion type

Stainless steel develops small deep holes.

Pitting corrosion is localized and dangerous.

Q4. Best preventive measure for galvanic corrosion?

Two dissimilar metals are connected in seawater.

Using a sacrificial anode protects the base metal.

Q5. Which protection method is used for underground pipelines?

Cathodic protection is widely used for buried pipelines.

Q6. Corrosion under bolts and gaskets is called?

Crevice corrosion occurs due to differential aeration.

Q7. Which is a dry corrosion example?

Dry corrosion occurs without moisture.
